# PRODUCT MARKET-FIT RESEARCH ANALYSIS

## CLIENT INTRODUCTION

**AirPure Innovations** is a startup born out of the air quality crisis in India , with 14 cities ranking among the world's top 20 most polluted urban centers. The company is in the early stages of product development and is unsure whether there is a strong , sustained demand for its air purifier product. Before committing to production and R&D ,they need to answer critical questions:

 1. What pollutants or particles should their air purifier target?

 2. What are the most essential features that should be incorporated into the air purifier?

 3. Which cities have the highest demand for air purifiers, and what is the market size in these regions?

 4. How can R&D be aligned with localized pollution patters?


COO of AirPure Innovations believes success lies in analyzing three key dimensions of the market. So he reached out to the analytics team, to assist in gathering insights for strategic decisions . The three dimensions to be analyzed are:

1. **Severity Mapping** : Identify cities experiencing persistent or worsening AQI(Air Quality Index) levels.
2. **Health Impact Correlation**: Quantify the health burden due to pollution and its impact on consumer's well-being.
3. **Demand Triggers**: Examine the relationship between pollution spikes and shifts in consumer behavior related to air purifier demand.

### Key Insights

#### 1. PM2.5 is the Primary Pollutant

Analysis shows that PM2.5 particles are the dominant pollutant across major cities, requiring high-efficiency particulate filtration.

#### 2. Pollution Patterns Vary by City


#### 3. Seasonal Pollution Peak

Pollution levels increases significantly during winter months ( October - January), particularly in northern India.
This indicates a clear seasonal demand window for air purifiers.

### Proposed Product - AirPure Smart Air Purifier

Based on the analysis, the proposed AirPure product includes:
- H13 HEPA filtration for fine particulate removal
- activated carbon filtration for gaseous pollutants
- real-time AQI monitoring
- automatic purification mode
- low-noise sleep mode( < 30 dB )
- filter replacement alerts

The purifier is designed to achieve 95% reduction in PM2.5 within 30 minutes in a 300 sq ft room with a CADR of approximately m³/hr.
